Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 17, 2026

Local image processing

purupuru maker is designed to process all editor image data locally in your browser. Image loading, mask painting, animation preview, and MP4/GIF export happen on your device. We do not upload, store, retain, or review your images, painted masks, or exported videos on a purupuru maker server.

Files you choose

You are responsible for the images you open in the editor. The browser may keep temporary local in-memory data while the page is open so the tool can render previews and exports. Closing or refreshing the page clears the editor session unless your browser keeps its own temporary cache.

Technical logs

When you visit the website, hosting providers and network services may receive standard technical information such as IP address, user agent, requested URL, and request time. These logs are used for security, diagnostics, and reliable delivery of the site. They do not include the image files, masks, or exported videos you create in the editor.

Analytics

The current app code does not include image-content analytics. If analytics are added later, they should not collect your selected files, file names, image content, masks, or exported videos.

Third-party services

The app runs in the browser and uses browser APIs such as Canvas, WebGL, and MediaRecorder. Your browser vendor and hosting provider may process data according to their own policies.

Changes

This policy may be updated as the product changes. The date above will be updated when material changes are made.
