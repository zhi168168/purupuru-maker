Are my images uploaded to purupuru maker?

No. purupuru maker is designed to process image editing work locally in your browser. Image loading, motion-mask painting, wobble preview, and MP4 or GIF export happen on your device.

What stays local

The selected image file, painted mask, preview canvas, and exported media are handled by browser APIs. The app code does not send those files to a purupuru maker server for editing or storage.

What website requests can still include

Like most websites, hosting and network services may receive standard technical request data such as IP address, user agent, requested URL, and request time. That delivery data is separate from the image content you edit in the canvas.

How to reduce risk while editing

Use images you have permission to edit, keep your browser updated, and avoid sharing exported animations publicly unless you have the right to do so. For the complete policy, read the purupuru maker privacy policy.
