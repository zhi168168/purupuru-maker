What is purupuru maker?

purupuru maker is a free browser-based image wobble maker that turns a still image into a soft, jelly-like wobble animation. This independent browser tool is inspired by the bouncy effect often used for character art, avatars, stickers, memes, and light image edits.

Where the idea comes from

The word purupuru describes a trembling or jiggling motion. In image editing, the idea is to make selected parts of a still picture gently bounce while the rest of the image stays more stable.

What purupuru maker does

The tool combines three steps in one page: upload an image, paint a motion mask over the parts that should move, and preview the wobble before export. This matches the way creators usually think about the effect: protect the stable base of the picture, then add motion only to flexible details.

Main features

  • Upload a PNG, JPEG, or WebP image directly in the browser.
  • Paint a motion mask over the areas that should wobble.
  • Choose brush thickness for precise edges or faster broad strokes.
  • Adjust strength, speed, spring, damping, and Motion over time for the wobble style.
  • Preview the motion in real time and export a short MP4 video or GIF.

Main workflow

The basic workflow is simple: keep the important base of the image steady, paint flexible details like hair, ribbons, sleeves, ears, stickers, or accessories, then tune the motion until it feels bouncy without looking distorted.

When to use it

purupuru maker is useful when you want a quick animated sticker, a subtle avatar movement, a playful meme, or a soft product-image motion without installing desktop animation software. For detailed steps, see how to use purupuru maker.
